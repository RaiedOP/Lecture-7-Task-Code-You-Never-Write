# Grading Policy (as given by the teacher)

| Category      | Weight | Special Rule                          |
|---------------|--------|----------------------------------------|
| Quizzes       | 15%    | Drop the single lowest quiz score      |
| Assignments   | 20%    | No drops                               |
| Midterm Exam  | 20%    | —                                      |
| Participation | 10%    | —                                      |
| Final Exam    | 35%    | Not yet taken                          |

**Target grade:** 85% (A-)

Notes:
- Each category score is first converted to a percentage
  (score / max_score), THEN the lowest-quiz-drop rule is applied
  within the Quizzes category before weighting.
- The weighted average of all 5 categories = final course grade.
